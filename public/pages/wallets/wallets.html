Convenience

Cryptocurrency has huge potential to be the most convenient way to pay for purchases and transfer currency. At the moment to transfer money from one parties wallet to another, all you have to do is send a specified amount of crypto to their public address where they will receive the crypto within minutes. To pay for a purchase in person or online, QR codes are a very popular and easy to use. Also if you want to send money to somebody using one currency such as the chinese Yuan or Bitcoin and they want to receive the money in another currency such as US dollars or Ether, this will one day be possible using blockchain technology such as the OmiseGo network.

Any transaction, transfer or exchange of currency is almost free on the blockchain because banks cannot take a cut.

Recoverable

A good wallet will have recovery features. This means that if the device that the wallet resides on goes missing, using a Recovery Passphrase and the same wallet or a wallet of the same specifications on a different device you can regain access to your funds.

The Recovery Passphrase link specifies concepts in terms of a Bitcoin wallet however, the information provided also pertains to other cryptocurrencies as well.

Responsibility

When you use a crypto wallet you have complete and ultimate control over your money. However this means you are also responsible for it's safety. Crypto wallets are a secure method of currency storage but before you commit to using one, make sure you are literate in it's use. Research the best wallet for your crypto, understand how to safely pay for items and transfer funds, always make sure you keep your recovery phrase a secret and that you know how to use it in the case that your wallet goes missing.
