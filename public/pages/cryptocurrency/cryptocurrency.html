Cryptocurrency

A digital store of value.


looks_one

Unique

Unlike currencies distributed by governments, a crypto (aka coin) does not have any physical form. Instead, it is represented by a unique code that cannot be copied or falsified. Very much like a serial number on a dollar bill.

trending_up

Deflationary

Limited supply and higher demand causes an increase in value.

When cryptocurrencies are created, the regulation of the supply (amount) is defined and in many cases a maximum amount of the supply is set. This causes there to be a limited amount of the currency in circulation and therefore if the demand grows so will the price and purchasing power of the currency. In contrast, currencies managed by central banks (private companies hired to regulate a country's currency), have no maximum supply. Central banks can print more money, which causes the currencies value to decrease and therefore inflation.

adb

Automated

Rules are defined and then the system runs itself.

Cryptocurrencies are defined by regulatory algorithms and are tracked using an underlying technology called the blockchain. This means that well made cryptocurrency can exist independently of a bank, and cannot be manipulated outside of the confines of the regulatory algorithms. As an added bonus, depending on the crypto, there is almost zero fees associated with transactions, transfers or conversions.

Digital

Accessible from anywhere and you are in complete control.

A crypto coin is a digital code and is stored on the blockchain and because of this it can be accessed using any digital device. If you own crypto you can accessed it on your computer, your phone, a usb drive and online using digital wallets. Do to the fact that it is stored on the blockchain it also means that you have absolute possession over your crypto, as long as you are the only one that knows your passphrase. In the current banking system you and not even your bank has possession over your money. The bank loans out your money to others and gives you a digital representation of your money through your online banking, debit card or credit cards.

Technology

The best technology managed and built by volunteers and/or foundations.

The financial system is a large machine built on the foundations of incredibly old technology. If the financial system needs to be adjusted or changed it can take years. Cryptocurrency is built on modern technology that is extremely fast and updated monthly. It is supported by an open source mindset that allows technology enthusiasts to share ideas and contribute to a variety of cryptos. This allows the best ideas to come to fruition at an incredibly fast rate.
