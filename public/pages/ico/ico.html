Initial Coin Offering

Raising money in order to build a product.


child_friendly

Inception

An ICO or Initial Coin Offering is the first time a cryptocurrency is offered to the public for purchase and is a method for a company to raise money, in order to develop the technology the currency will support.

* A Cryptocurrency is a store of value and can represent any asset. It can be a currency, represent membership, be a share in a company, etc. *

flight_takeoff

Raise

The company that offered the coin for purchase will be the recipient of the funds raise from the ICO. These funds will be used to enable the company to develop their product.

donut_small

Hold

A company will often hold onto a portion of the coins and not offer them to the public for purchase. This means that the company has a personal stake in their products success. If their product is successful and therefore more people begin to use their coin, the price of the coin will go up making their holdings more valuable. However, if the product is unsuccessful, their holdings will lose their value.

Distribution

ICO's are offered on online cryptocurrency exchanges, making them very easy to purchase from. Sometimes the company will also offer a Pre ICO which will allow the very first people to purchase the coin at a discounted price.

traffic

Regulation

Currently there is very little ICO specific regulation in countries. However, companies that hold ICO's are still bound to the laws and regulations of their country. For example in many countries if a company were to offer an ICO with no intention of fulfilling what they promise, the country regulators may be able to charge them with fraud.
